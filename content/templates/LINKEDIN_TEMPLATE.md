# LinkedIn Post Template

Structure (do not include these labels in the actual post):

---

[HOOK — 1–2 lines. A moment, a realization, or a provocative opinion. No "I'm excited to share."]

[CONTEXT — 2–3 lines. What you were doing, what question came up, why it matters to you personally.]

[THE INSIGHT — 4–6 lines. The core thing you learned or built. Be specific. Name the concept. Show one concrete detail — a number, a code snippet, a surprising result.]

[YOUR OPINION — 2–3 lines. What you actually think about this. Where it falls short. What surprises you. This is the line people share.]

[CALL TO ACTION — 1 question directed at your audience. Something you genuinely want to know.]

[LINK LINE — "Full article + notebook: [dev.to link]"]

Hashtags (always last, never inline): #AIAlignment #Anthropic #LLM #BuildingInPublic #MachineLearning #Python #Claude

---

## Notes

- 200–300 words total
- Hashtags on a separate line at the very end
- The opinion line is the most important — it should be specific enough to be disagreeable
- The question should be open enough that both technical and non-technical people can answer
